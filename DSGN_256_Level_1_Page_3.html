Excerpt from
Sam Raimis
Spider-Man 3, 2007:
"Uncle Ben meant the world to us.
 
But he wouldn't want us living one second with revenge in our hearts.
 
It's like a poison.
 
It can - It can take you over.
 
Before you know it, turn us into something ugly."


~Aunt May, played by Rosemary Harris

Here is a link to the original movie clip that this excerpt was pulled from. Skip to the 1:11 mark to hear the exact quote from Aunt May.
This is one of my favorite quotes from a Spider-Man movie for several reasons:
  1. It represents a heavy moment of foreshadowing in the film, wherein Peter's will is tested as he chooses between using his newfound, mysterious black suit, or staying true to himself and the classic red-and-blue outfit.

  2. It signifies the beginning of Peter's descent into a destructive state of mind and way of living that effectively pushes away everyone he cares about, making him miserable—a result of the alien symbiote's dark influence on his character that amplifies his personal faults and baggage.

  3. It encapsulates the film's theme of how our decisions can have monumental effects on ourselves and those around us. After all, one of the slogans touted on the movie posters was: "Every hero has a choice."
